Resources

A curated collection of origami instruction and inspiration — for folders of every level.

Start Here

Learning to Fold

Origami Resource Center

Step-by-step diagrams for beginners through advanced. One of the most comprehensive free collections online.

Origami Instructions

Clear photo-based instructions for hundreds of models. Great for beginners who prefer pictures over diagrams.

Tadashi Mori (YouTube)

High-quality video tutorials ranging from simple to advanced. Exceptional production and clear instruction.

Community & Organizations

The Origami World

OrigamiUSA

The national organization for origami in America. Annual convention, diagrams library, and membership community.

Gilad's Origami Page

An encyclopedic database of origami books, designers, and models. The definitive reference for serious folders.

Robert J. Lang

Website of one of the foremost origami artists and theorists in the world. Includes TreeMaker software.

Arnold Tubis

The archive of our beloved co-coordinator — boxes, bowls, books, and a lifetime of folding.

British Origami Society

One of the oldest origami societies in the world, with a rich publication history and active membership.

r/origami

An active community for sharing folds, asking questions, and discovering new models.

Member Recommendations

Books We Love

Florence Temko
Origami for Beginners

One of many beloved books by our co-founder, making origami accessible to all ages.

Robert J. Lang
Origami Design Secrets

The definitive book on the mathematical principles behind designing original origami models.

Peter Engel
Origami from Angelfish to Zen

A beautiful exploration of the philosophy, art, and craft of paper folding.

Eric Gjerde
Origami Tessellations

The go-to guide for tessellation folding — geometric patterns that create stunning surfaces.
